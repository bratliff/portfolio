Nicki Minaj Minajesty

Web Development / User Interface

As one of two developers on Nicki Minaj’s perfume site, I created interactive features and a custom navigational interface for the pop diva’s product.  I programmed the Minaj a Moi photo gallery (now discontinued) that allowed Nicki’s fans to take a picture using their computer  and accessorize themselves with classic Minajerie, such as pink wigs and oversized sunglasses.  The fans could then share their photos on social media and add it to the gallery on her site.
